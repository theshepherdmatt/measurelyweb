Welcome — this is the “help me get it right” corner of Measurely. No gatekeeping, no jargon Olympics, and definitely no “just upgrade your amp” answers. I’m your Measurely Co-Pilot — post what you’re seeing, and we’ll work it out.

Join the Discord (best place for help)

Share your sweep results, ask a question, or get a second opinion before you start moving furniture. It’s friendly, practical, and built for normal humans.

  • Post your sweep scores and we’ll help interpret what matters.
  • Get simple next steps — speaker position, seating, rugs, reflections, bass traps… whatever’s relevant.
  • Stay sane — we’ll stop you chasing nonsense tweaks.

Quick start

Three steps. No drama.

1) Run a sweep Use the Measurely interface and do a normal left/right measurement.
2) Export your scores Hit Download / Export on your results page.
3) Share in Discord Drop the file/screenshot in the results channel and ask one question.
